## Gaeilge

Is mise **Éimí Mhic an Ridire**. Bean Éireannach atá i mo chónaí in Oirthuaisceart na hAlban.

Tá mo chuid oibre bunaithe ar inrochtaineacht dhigiteach fhíor: mar úsáideoir cathaoireacha rothaí neuroéagsúil, tuigim ó thaithí féin cé chomh tábhachtach is atá dearadh a chuireann daoine i gcroílár an scéil.

Rinne mé staidéar ar Ríomhaireacht & TF leis an Ollscoil Oscailte sa Ríocht Aontaithe, agus táim anois ag déanamh Léann Éireannach in Ollscoil na Gaillimhe.

Creidim i ndroichid, ní i mballaí. Cuirim le tionscadail foinse oscailte agus le pobail eolais mar Wikimedia Commons agus OpenStreetMap.

## English

I am **Éimí Mhic an Ridire**, an Irish woman living in North-East Scotland.

My work is grounded in genuinely accessible digital experiences. As a neurodivergent wheelchair user, I care deeply about design that serves real people.

I previously studied Computing & IT with The Open University and now study Irish Studies with the University of Galway.

I believe in building bridges, not walls, and I contribute to open-source and open-knowledge communities including Wikimedia Commons and OpenStreetMap.
