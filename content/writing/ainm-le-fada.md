## Gaeilge

Is minic a fheicim m’ainm gan an síneadh fada. Ní mionrud é sin.

Is cuid de m’aitheantas agus de mo theanga féin é: **Éimí** atá orm, le fada.

## English

I often see my name written without the fada. That is not a small detail.

It is part of my identity and language: my name is **Éimí**, with a fada.
