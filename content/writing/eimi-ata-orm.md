## Gaeilge

A chairde, tá sé simplí: **Éimí** atá orm. Le fada, le meas.

Má fheiceann tú “Eimi”, is botún é. Is leor ceartú beag cineálta.

## English

Friends, it is simple: my name is **Éimí**.

If you see “Eimi”, that is a mistake. A gentle correction is enough.
