Bringing a new design to eim.ie for 2025, this site is now accessible on my older hardware with Classilla and TenFourFox.

I'm excited to explore the PowerPC generation of Apple Computers with my G3 iMac and iBook and other PPC Apple Hardware.

I want to give an early mention to [Macintosh Garden](https://www.macintoshgarden.org/ "Macintosh Garden | Celebrating Macintosh Abandonware"), [Sloop](https://directory.sloop.nz "Sloop Directory"), and [The Bookyard](https://www.thebookyard.com "The Bookyard | Apple Replacement Parts in the UK"), all of which have been great resources to get started.

I'm going to be looking at exactly how useful these 20+ year old machines are in the modern age, (hint: a lot!) as well as developing new applications for Mac OS 9.2, 10.0, and 10.4.

I'll also be posting about my other personal interests, including the Irish language, European Politics, and much more.

So come along for the ride, making sure to keep your arms, legs, hands, feet, and head inside the vehicle at all times.

Let's go!
