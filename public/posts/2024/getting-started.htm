Getting Started
by Éimí Mhic an Ridire  |  07 December 2024

Are you sitting comfortably?

... Good then we'll begin.

Bringing a new design to eim.ie for 2025, this site is now accessible on my older hardware with Classilla and TenFourFox.

I'm excited to explore the PowerPC generation of Apple Computers with my G3 iMac and iBook and other PPC Apple Hardware.

:: A few early shout-outs ::

Macintosh Garden — celebrating Mac abandonware. An invaluable resource.

Sloop — the indie web directory, and what this site is built on.

The Bookyard — Apple replacement parts in the UK. Get yourself some RAM upgrades!

I'll be looking at exactly how useful these 20+ year old machines are in the modern age (hint: a lot!), as well as developing new applications for Mac OS 9.2, 10.0, and 10.4.

I'll also be posting about my other personal interests, including the Irish language, European Politics, and much more.

So come along for the ride, making sure to keep your arms, legs, hands, feet, and head inside the vehicle at all times.

Let's go!
